About

Ori was created for anyone new to freelancing who is not used to the responsibilities entailed in this labor; Such as knowing what to charge your clients, as well as, and writing up proposals, contracts, invoices. Thus, its ment to help them learn the ropes to freelancing as they begin their career.

FEATURES

Templates

Professional looking templates that can later be saved and exported as a pdf. These templates will be set for : proposals, contracts and invoices.

Time Tracker

Prices are usually established on the length of the project but actually knowing how long it takes can be a bit tricky at first. Set the timer to help you get an idea of how long a project takes!

Calculator

Not sure what to charge your clients? Based off of some certain key values, Ori has a built in calculator to help you get an idea of what to charge.

Directory

An easy to use directoy where key project information will be stored. Each projects will be linked to a client. All this information will be stored in a database to keep track of your clients and projects progress.

TECHNOLOGY

Ori will be initally design to be a desktop app because most of its features require more the use of a computer over a phone. For example, it would be confortable to edit contracts and proposals on the computer rather than on a phone. I have also gone with a LAMP stack approach due to its relational database.
